### Why this exists

A CLA gives the Project the legal clarity to accept contributions and
to keep licensing options open as the Project matures. Without a CLA,
every contributor would retain copyright over their contributions, and
the Project could not relicense or dual-license without tracking down
and re-asking each past contributor.

The ASQL CLA follows the Apache Software Foundation model closely and
is intentionally short. If You have concerns about any clause, please
open a discussion on the repository before signing.
